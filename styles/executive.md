# Executive Style Guide

Use this when the reader needs the decision, impact, risks, and next action
without implementation-level detail.

## Rules

- Lead with the answer or recommendation.
- Keep context to the minimum required for the decision.
- Group detail under impact, risk, evidence, and next action.
- Avoid raw logs unless they are the artifact being reviewed.
- Include full links or paths when they are needed for follow-up.
